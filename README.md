## 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm an **Assistant Professor** in the Department of Computer Engineering (Smart Security) at **Gachon University**.  
My research focuses on **system/software security** — finding and exploiting vulnerabilities in complex software systems.

> 🔍 I'm looking for motivated **graduate and undergraduate students** interested in fuzzing, symbolic execution, and vulnerability analysis. Feel free to [contact me](mailto:shjeon90@gachon.ac.kr)!

- 🏫 **Affiliation:** Gachon University, College of IT Convergence
- 🏢 **Office:** AI Building #749
- 🎓 **Ph.D.** in Information Security, Korea University (2018–2022)
